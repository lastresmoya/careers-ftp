Grow With Us

Cultivating a workplace that supports professional development is one of many ways that we invest in our employees. Throughout your career at FT Partners, we create opportunities for you to grow within our organization. You will have access to the best internal training tools and external training programs. All it takes is new learning experiences and knowledge to open the door for career advancement opportunities with FT Partners and other members of the group.

Summer Internship

Our 10-week Summer internship program takes place in early June through early August each summer. Summer Analysts and Associates work on live deals, alongside our deal teams and directly with senior investment bankers on a variety of responsibilities, such as, M&A, capital raising transactions, financial modeling, company valuation, due diligence, strategic analyses of companies, and much more.

Mentorship Program

As an apprenticeship based business, we understand the importance of both on the job coaching and mentoring. Each Analyst and Associate is matched with a senior banker to serve as a Mentor. The Mentor is an experienced and trusted adviser for guidance on career development.

Professional Development

We offer comprehensive training programs across levels and sectors to ensure our bankers have all the tools and resources to be successful. Additionally, we strive to provide a highly collaborative and collegial environment that creates opportunities for each and every individual to flourish.

Perks and Benefits

We want people to thrive at FT Partners; we believe you do your best work when you feel your best. Here are a just few benefits we're proud our team.

Health Care

Our employees are entitled to a variety of healthcare insurance plans to choose from, covering medical, dental and vision. Our healthcare packages cover you and your partner and your children..

Dependent Care

Flexible Spending Account (FSA) Plans help you pay for eligible out-of-pocket and dependent day care expenses, like pre-school.

Retirement Savings Plans

Our employees benefit from programs such as 401(k) savings plan in the U.S. and pension schemes in the U.K.

Pre-Tax Commuter Benefits

Our commuter benefit plan allows you to set aside pre-tax dollars to pay for eligible commuting costs, such as expenses for parking, public transit and alternative forms of transportation.

Life And Disability Insurance

We have a suite of life and disability options for you and your family.

Pet Insurance

We offer discounted pet insurance via MetLife, covering both major medical and pet wellness programs.
